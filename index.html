Suggested Tips

 

PRE-RUN (1-3 hours before)

  1. Focus on easily digestible carbohydrates and moderates protein for sustained energy.
  2. For examples, banana with peanut butter, oatmeal with berries, whole wheat toast with eggs.
  3. If your run is longer (over 60 minutes), consider a small energy bar or gel 30 minutes beforehand.

 

HYDRATION

  1. Throughout the day, aim for consistent water intake.
  2. Before run, drink 16-20 ounces of water to ensure proper hydration.

 

MID-RUN (During long runs)

  1. Replenish electrolytes and carbohydrates to maintain energy levels.
  2. Aim for 30-60 grams of carbohydrates per hour. Consider sports drinks, gels, chews, or fruits like bananas or dates.

 

POST-RUN (Within 1 hours)

  1. This is the prime window for muscle recovery.
